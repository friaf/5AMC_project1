5am Club in six steps:

  1. Rise at 5 am
  2. Spend 20 minutes on exercise
  3. Invest next 20 working on your plans/dreams
  4. Devote last 20 to learning
  5. Run this 20 / 20 / 20 formula for 66 days
  6. Share your progress on social media

The benefits of waking up at 5 am

  • You'll have time to connect with yourself, your purpose, and your High Power
  • You'll have time for personal development and progress toward goals
  • You'll get into a peak state, which will influence everything else you do that day
  • By putting first things first, you'll have “moral authority” around your loved ones
  • You'll have made progress, which will increase your confidence
  • You'll feel more motivated
  • You increase your standards for everything else you do
  • You'll sleep better
  • You'll make more money
  • You'll be a new (and better) person
